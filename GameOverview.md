# The Twilight Stronghold: Game Overview

> "Build by Day. Survive by Night. Farm Forever."

## 1. The Core Concept
**The Twilight Stronghold** is a **Hybrid Survival RPG** on Roblox that bridges the gap between chill, persistent collection games and high-stakes roguelite survival.

*   **The Vibe:** Cozy medieval guild meets terrifying night survival.
*   **The Hook:** Everything you build and collect in the **Lobby** makes you stronger in the **War Zone**, but the War Zone is where the real glory (and loot) lies.

---

## 2. The Mechanics (The "Split Loop")

### Phase A: The Lobby (Persistent Progression)
*   **Role:** The "Hub" and "Safe Zone". Your progress here is permanent.
*   **Gameplay Style:** Low-stress, social, farming-focused (inspired by *Fisch*).
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **Resource Gathering:** Fishing in the guild lake, mining in the quarry, chopping ancient trees.
    *   **Crafting & Upgrading:** Use resources to forge **Loadout Items** (weapons, armor, base blueprints).
    *   **Meta-Progression:** Unlock permanent Class Upgrades (Warrior, Mage, Builder) using "Diamonds" earned from survival.
    *   **Sanctuary Zones:** Dedicated areas for AFK farming (lower rates, but automatic) to respect player time.

### Phase B: The War Zone (Session Survival)
*   **Role:** The "Instance" and "Challenge". Resets every match.
*   **Gameplay Style:** High-intensity, cooperative tower defense (inspired by *99 Nights*).
*   **The Challenge:** Survive as many nights as possible against waves of monsters.
*   **The Day/Night Cycle:**

*   **The Stakes:** If you die, the session ends. You keep your Meta-Currency (Diamonds), but the base is gone.

### Phase C: The Meta-Link (Connecting the Two)
*   **The Loadout System:** You don't go into battle empty-handed.
    *   **Bring 1 Weapon:** (e.g., *Void Slayer Greatsword*) crafted in the Lobby.
    *   **Bring 1 Base Kit:** (e.g., *Tier 3 Watchtower Blueprint*) to start the match with a massive advantage.
